In a vignere_cipher our job is the Use the message and key to 
pull our message out of the left column of upperCase letters.

The process looks like this:

   
	the first letter 'l' is connected to 't' from the key.
	Find T and go down the column until you reach L. 
	The corresponding left hand side letter will be our
	message.
